Computer Engineering And Students Association.

Computer engineering and students association (CEnSA), is an organisation of computer department students in Govt.Residential women's polytechnic college.

The department of Computer Engineering is one of the best departments that has always increased the glory of GRWPC.Over the years, the department has been successfull in preparing young minds to be outstanding engineers who are prominet in academics and related skills. The department moulds young outstanding engineers who are academically strong in both theory and practical.

It organizes supplementary programs, which include Paper Presentions, Technical seminars & Mock Interviews to prepare the student for a career in the computing field or for industry acceptable.
